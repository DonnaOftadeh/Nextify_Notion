## Idea Problem Snapshot

Pet owners face a fragmented market when seeking products, services, and information for their pets. This problem is amplified by the lack of a unified platform in Berlin, leading to wasted time and potential compromise in pet care quality. As pet ownership increases, the need for a comprehensive solution grows, making now the opportune time to address this gap.

## Idea Brainstorm Uses

Pet owners need a centralized platform for managing all aspects of their pet's life. This platform will need to address the fragmented nature of current solutions. Here are some possible uses and workaround behaviors:

*   **Finding pet sitters:** Currently, pet owners rely on word-of-mouth, local listings, or general classifieds. They then interview and vet each sitter individually.
*   **Booking pet-friendly travel:** Researching pet-friendly hotels and flights is time-consuming. Owners often check multiple websites and call ahead to confirm pet policies.
*   **Locating specialized pet products:** Finding unique or specialized pet products requires searching various online stores and local boutiques. Comparison shopping is difficult.
*   **Accessing veterinary information:** Owners search online for health information, but it is often generic or unreliable. There is a need for trusted veterinary advice.
*   **Managing pet records:** Pet owners use paper records, scattered digital documents, or various apps to track vaccinations, medical history, and appointments.
*   **Connecting with other pet owners:** Currently, connections are usually limited to local dog parks or social media groups. The existing options aren't specific enough.
*   **Finding local pet-related events:** Discovering pet-friendly events in Berlin requires scouring local event calendars and social media.
*   **Comparing pet insurance options:** Researching and comparing pet insurance plans involves visiting multiple websites and requesting quotes.

### How RICE Was Computed

- Reach = people per week.
- Impact = 1–5 scale.
- Confidence = 0–1.
- Effort = person-weeks.
- RICE = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort.
